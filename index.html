Get Started

Install the Home Assistant custom integration, connect the iOS app, and pick your metrics.

  1. Install integrations: Add the HealthSync HA custom component to Home Assistant or connect Google Sheets.
  2. Copy your webhook: In HA, open the HealthSync HA config to get the webhook ID/URL (or use your Sheets endpoint from the app).
  3. Configure the app: In the iOS app, paste the target URL, select metrics, and tap Authorize & Start Sync.
  4. Automate or log: Use HA sensors in automations, or capture a timeline in Sheets for trending.
Tip: You can run both integrations in parallel—automate in HA while logging to Sheets.
