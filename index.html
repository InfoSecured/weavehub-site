Products

Two iOS apps, built for speed and clarity.

HealthSync HA

Apple HealthKit → Home Assistant + Google Sheets. Webhook-based, local-first, no polling.

  • Metrics: heart rate, HRV, SpO₂, energy, distance, hydration, BP, more.
  • Targets: Home Assistant sensors, Sheets logging.
  • Built for automations and journaling in parallel.

BulletMatch (Scott's Guide)

A precision-focused reference for reloaders. Quickly search and compare bullets by caliber, manufacturer, weight, or construction type.

  • Catalog of published specs: weight, diameter, length, BC, design traits.
  • Side-by-side comparisons and saved sets with personal notes.
  • Search/filter by caliber, brand, construction; optional inventory tracking.

Important: BulletMatch is a reference tool only—no load data, charges, seating depth, or safety guidance. Always use official manuals and verify through manufacturers.

Get Started

Pick your app, point it at your target, and start streaming or researching.

  1. HealthSync HA (automation): Add the HA custom component or connect Sheets. Copy your webhook URL.
  2. Configure the app: In iOS, paste the target URL, select metrics, and tap Authorize & Start Sync.
  3. Automate or log: Use HA sensors in automations, or capture a timeline in Sheets for trending.
  4. BulletMatch (reference): Search, filter, and compare bullets; save comparison sets and notes.
Tip: You can run both integrations in parallel—automate in HA while logging to Sheets.
