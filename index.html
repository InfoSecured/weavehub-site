Get Started

Install the Home Assistant custom integration, connect the iOS app, and pick your metrics.

  1. Install the integration: Add the HealthSync HA custom component to Home Assistant.
  2. Copy your webhook: In HA, open the HealthSync HA config to get the webhook ID/URL.
  3. Configure the app: In the iOS app, paste the webhook URL, select metrics, and tap Authorize & Start Sync.
  4. Automate: Use the new sensors in HA automations (e.g., adjust climate based on resting HR).
